PiPi Focus Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

PiFl Labs Co., Ltd. ("Company", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use the mobile application PiPi Focus ("App"). For matters not covered here, our general Privacy Policy applies.

1. Purpose of Processing

  • App functionality: Pomodoro timer, statistics, widgets, notifications, and user preferences
  • Advertising: Ad serving and personalization via Google AdMob
  • In-app purchases: Premium feature purchases and receipt validation
  • Customer support: Receiving and responding to email inquiries

2. Information We Collect

The App does not require account registration. We collect the following:

Category Items Collection Method
Advertising Identifier Ad ID (IDFA/AAID), coarse location (country level), device info (OS, model, language) Automatic via Google AdMob SDK (with consent)
In-App Purchases Purchase receipt, transaction ID Apple App Store / Google Play billing
App Usage Data Timer history, statistics, preferences, gameplay state Stored locally on device (no external transmission)
Email Inquiries Email address, name (optional), inquiry content Direct email submission

3. Retention Period

  • Advertising identifier and ad data: As per Google AdMob policy
  • In-app purchase receipts: 5 years (Korean Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce)
  • Local app data: Until the user deletes the App or resets the data
  • Email inquiry records: 3 years after resolution

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. Processing Entrustment

Processor Purpose Retention
Google LLC (AdMob) Mobile ad serving and ad identifier processing Until contract termination
Apple Inc. iOS in-app purchase processing Until contract termination
Google LLC (Google Play) Android in-app purchase processing Until contract termination

6. Advertising Identifier and Tracking Consent

The App displays advertisements via Google AdMob and may collect advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID) for this purpose.

  • iOS users: On first launch, you will see Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt to choose whether to allow tracking. If denied, only non-personalized ads will be shown.
  • Android users: You can opt out via Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of ad personalization".

For details on Google AdMob's data processing, see the Google Privacy Policy.

7. App Permissions

The App may request the following permissions. Each can be denied, though doing so may limit related functionality.

  • Notifications: Pomodoro session end, break reminders, and other timer notifications
  • App Tracking Transparency (iOS): Tracking consent for ad relevance
  • Microphone (iOS, optional): System requirement for audio playback features

8. Data Destruction

  • Procedure: Unnecessary personal data is destroyed after approval by the Data Protection Officer.
  • Method: Electronic files are permanently deleted using non-recoverable methods. Local app data is removed when the user uninstalls the App.

  2. Right to consent to and determine the scope of processing
  3. Right to access and request information about your personal data
  4. Right to request correction, deletion, suspension, or destruction
  5. Right to seek redress for damages caused by processing
