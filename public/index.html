02 / The Name

Two syllables. One dumb joke.

Captain's Log · Entry 001Seoul, 26° ENE

We wanted a name that was half math, half nonsense. Pi for the endless loop of software. Fl for the framework that lets one codebase fly across every platform.

Put them together and you get PiFl — which, read fast, sounds like a pirate with a lisp. Good. That's on-brand.

Our first mate is a parrot who codes. She has fourteen hats. She wears them all at once, somehow.

— the crew
