Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

PiFl Labs Co., Ltd. ("Company", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use our website at pifl-labs.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

Category Items Collection Method
Email Inquiries Email address, name (optional), inquiry content Direct email submission
Website Access IP address, browser type, access timestamp Automatic collection

2. Purpose of Processing

  • Inquiry handling: Receiving customer inquiries via email, contacting for verification, and notifying processing results
  • Recruitment inquiries: Receiving and responding to employment-related inquiries
  • Website operation: Analyzing access statistics and improving services

3. Retention Period

  • Email inquiry records: 3 years after inquiry resolution (Korean Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce)
  • Website access logs: 3 months (Korean Protection of Communications Secrets Act)

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent in accordance with Articles 17 and 18 of the Korean Personal Information Protection Act.

5. Processing Entrustment

For service operation, we entrust personal data processing to the following providers:

Processor Purpose Retention
Google LLC (Firebase) Website hosting and infrastructure Until contract termination
Notion Labs, Inc. Internal data management Until contract termination

6. Data Destruction

  • Procedure: Unnecessary personal data is destroyed after approval by the Data Protection Officer.
  • Method: Electronic files are permanently deleted using non-recoverable methods. Paper documents are shredded or incinerated.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  1. Right to be informed about the processing of your personal data
  2. Right to consent to and determine the scope of processing
  3. Right to access and request information about your personal data
  4. Right to request correction, deletion, suspension, or destruction of your data
  5. Right to seek redress for damages caused by processing

You may exercise these rights via written request or email, and we will respond without delay.

8. Security Measures

  • Access control and permission management
  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Security headers (CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options, etc.)
  • Access logging and retention

9. Cookies

We currently do not use cookies or other automatic personal data collection devices. If we begin to do so in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.
